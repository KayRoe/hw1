Getting to know eachother

My name is Senait which means good things or blessings in my language.

I am Habesha, which is a general term we use to call our people in Ethiopia and Eritrea.

I grew up in Nairobi, Kenya. I like to paint, swim, read and meditate when I have time.

I also like to travel. I paint abstract pieces as well as landscape. My favourite author is Paulo Coelho.
